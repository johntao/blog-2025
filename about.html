Philosophy

This blog is a happy land for a developer who have been overwhelmed by the front-end and AI technology, and whoose only purpose for now is to find his inner peace in the crispy TUI world. Lynx FTW!

Pros & Cons

  • Pros:
  • NO MORE bombarded by new JS frameworks
  • NO MORE bothered by my ill CSS skills
  • Actually spending my time on making content
  • All the goodness you can get using low-tech (a11y, fast)
  • A chance to rethink the essence of web browsing and blogging
  • Keep myself away from implementing unnecessary features
  • Cons:
  • Lynx does not simply display images and videos
  • Migrating old blog posts might be a pain in the ass
  • Hyperlink is the only way to handle navigation
  • Less attractive (I guess)
