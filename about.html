About This Blog

Philosophy

This blog is built with pure HTML, following the principles of web minimalism and universal accessibility. No CSS, no JavaScript, just semantic markup that works everywhere.

Why Lynx-Compatible?

By designing for Lynx, the text-based browser, we ensure that this site is:

  • Accessible to everyone, regardless of their device or browser
  • Fast to load, even on slow connections
  • Privacy-respecting with no tracking scripts
  • Simple to maintain and archive
  • Compatible with screen readers and assistive technologies

About the Author

I'm a software developer and web enthusiast who believes in the power of simplicity. This blog is my experiment in creating content that prioritizes substance over style.

Technical Details

This blog is:

  • Static HTML files hosted on a simple web server
  • No databases, no server-side processing
  • No cookies, no tracking, no analytics
  • Fully bookmarkable with clean URLs
  • Navigable with keyboard shortcuts
